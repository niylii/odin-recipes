Waldorf Salad

Description :

Waldorf salad is a classic dish that combines crisp fruits and veggies in a creamy mayonnaise dressing. Created in 1896 by Oscar Tschirky at the Waldorf-Astoria in New York, this fruit and nut salad typically doesn’t include meat, though chicken can be added for a complete meal. Key ingredients include mayonnaise, white sugar, lemon juice, salt, apples, celery, walnuts, and optional raisins. For a heartier dish, consider adding diced, roasted chicken and serving it over lettuce for an elegant touch. To prepare, simply mix the dressing, stir in the other ingredients, and chill until ready to serve. Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days.

Ingredients :

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples — peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins (Optional)

Steps :

  • Step 1:

    Gather all ingredients.

  • Step 2:

    Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt together in a serving bowl.

  • Step 3:

    Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
